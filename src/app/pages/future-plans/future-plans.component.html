Future Plans

The Executive Committee has envisioned to transform the Mandir into a cultural heritage center by 2023 with following goals:

  • Daily prayer services.
  • Cultural Educational for education for all promoting the Vaidik philosophy of humanity as a religion.
  • Place for social gathering and exchange of social ideas.

For this goal we will need to re-design and build a bigger Temple with a projected cost of $400,000. Please donate generously towards this cause.
