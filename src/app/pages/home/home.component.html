Regular Service

Service is held every Sunday 9.00 AM to 11.30 AM. Prayer Service includes Meditation (Sandhya) followed by Hawan & Satsang.

We are a 100% volunteer operated organization and do not charge money for Sanskars and Prayer Services.

Special services can be arranged on request. Purohits available for Sanskars and other occassions.
