Humanitarian Work


Parbatie Etwaroo leads the food drive, wherein canned food is collected and then delivered to community food pantries feeding the homeless and under-privileged.

Parbatie Etwaroo leads the clothing drive, wherein new & lightly used clothes are collected and then distributed to the homeless and under-privileged.

Under the leadership of Pt. Suresh Sugrim, New Jersey Arya Samaj started a Humanitarian Mission in Guyana with the sole purpose of helping the underprivileged get back on their feet and lead a life of dignity. For more details please visit Humanitarian Mission of Guyana.
